## FAQs (Add to Q&A Section)

### Q: Is French Riviera Golf free to use?
Yes, French Riviera Golf is completely free to join and use. Create an account, browse courses, find playing partners, and join games at no cost.

### Q: How do I find playing partners on the French Riviera?
Simply create a free account, then either browse "Open Games" to join existing tee times, or post your own availability. You can filter by course, date, skill level, and preferred playing style. Once matched, you can chat with your group to coordinate.

### Q: What golf courses are listed on French Riviera Golf?
We feature 24 golf courses across the CÃ´te d'Azur, including Monte-Carlo Golf Club, Royal Mougins, Terre Blanche, Cannes-Mougins, Golf de Saint-Tropez, and many more. Each course has detailed information, photos, facilities, and user reviews.

### Q: Can visitors/tourists use French Riviera Golf?
Absolutely! French Riviera Golf is perfect for visitors looking to play while on holiday. Find local players to join, discover the best courses, and get insider tips. Many of our members enjoy meeting golfers from around the world.

### Q: What skill levels can join?
All skill levels are welcome - from beginners to scratch golfers. When creating or joining a game, you can specify your handicap and preferred playing pace. Our matching helps you find compatible partners.

### Q: Do you book tee times?
We connect you with playing partners, but tee time bookings are made directly with each golf course. We provide contact details and links to each course's booking system on their profile page.

### Q: Is the site available in French?
Yes! French Riviera Golf is bilingual. The site automatically detects your language preference, or you can switch between English and French.
